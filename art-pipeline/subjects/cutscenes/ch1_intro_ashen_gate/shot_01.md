# Cutscene shot: Ashen Gate arrival (chapter 1 intro hook)

A wide cinematic cutscene frame of four main characters standing at the threshold of a burnt-out frontier gate ruin, looking at the destruction ahead. Same illustrated world as the project's character roster splash and Town hub baseline. Painterly anime cinematic style — narrative gacha-banner tone, NOT photoreal, NOT concept-art realism. 16:9 wide.

## Composition

Camera: medium 3/4 view from slight side, the 4 main lead characters in mid-ground (their backs partially visible toward us), facing the burnt gate ahead. The destruction is the subject, the characters are the witnesses.

- **Center-foreground**: 4 lead character standees from 3/4 back view, varied heights and silhouettes:
  - **Dawn Priest 단린** (red hair + warm robe + sword) — center-left, slightly forward
  - **Pack Raider 이빨바람** (leather + bow + animal-tooth) — center-right
  - **Grave Hexer 묵향** (cool pale robe + book + violet hood) — back-left
  - **Echo Savant 공한** (white + violet lattice motif + bow) — back-right
- **Mid-ground**: a burnt frontier gate ruin (Castle_Gate vendor asset reference) — half-collapsed, dark scorch marks, broken stone pillars on either side, faint smoke still curling up from charred ground. Solarum sunburst sigil on the gate is **faded and partially burned away**
- **Background-far**: Autumn pine forest silhouette beyond the gate, leaves curled brown/black from heat damage, a few standing dead trees, distant ridge under overcast haze
- **Foreground-ground**: scorched earth, ash, scattered burnt debris (broken planks, charred fabric, a dropped weapon)
- **Sky**: overcast haze, washed-out warm grey-orange (not a healthy dusk — atmospheric scar)

## Mood

**Cold horror discovery.** The 4 characters stand silent at the threshold. The mood is "what happened here?" — not battle-ready, but witnessing. Dust still in the air. No bodies visible (mood, not gore). The Solarum sunburst sigil burned out suggests "their own kind was attacked, or attacked others — unclear". This is the chapter 1 hook moment.

## Lighting

- Overcast haze (no direct sun) — flat atmospheric lighting
- Subtle warm hint from smoldering embers still glowing in scorched ground (small pockets of warm orange)
- Cool ash-grey dominant
- Character figures lit by ambient + faint warm undertone from embers near foreground

## Palette

- Scorched earth-black + ash-grey (foreground) — dominant
- Burnt umber + char-black (gate ruin) — accent
- Faded solarum sigil (warm gold burned to grey-brown)
- Autumn pine silhouette (curled brown, dead orange) — background
- Hazy grey-orange sky (atmospheric scar) — top
- Character palettes per roster (each lead reads as their distinct silhouette)

## Style continuity

Same painterly anime brushwork as character roster + hub baseline. Mood is darker than hub (overcast vs dusk warm), but stylization vocabulary identical — anime-painted environment + figures, NOT realistic destruction photography.

## What this is NOT

- NOT a battle scene mid-action — quiet witness moment
- NOT character close-up — 4 standees at distance
- NOT photoreal fire / smoke / destruction
- NOT gore (no bodies, just material destruction)
- NOT bright daylight (overcast scar atmosphere)
- NOT a different stylization from hub / roster baseline
- NOT heroic pose (quiet observation, characters partly turned to look)
- NOT magical glowing effects (only natural ember warm hint)
