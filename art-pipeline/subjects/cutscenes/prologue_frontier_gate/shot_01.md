```prompt
# Cutscene shot: Frontier Gate crossing (campaign prologue closing)

A wide cinematic cutscene frame of two main characters walking through a heavy wooden frontier gate at dusk, leaving the frontier village behind, stepping into wilderness beyond. Same illustrated world as the project's character roster splash and the Town hub baseline (refs attached as visual baseline). Painterly anime cinematic style — gacha-banner narrative cutscene tone, NOT photoreal, NOT concept-art realism. 16:9 wide.

## Composition

Camera: slight low-angle 3/4 view from inside the gate looking out, the two characters mid-stride through the open gateway. The gate frames the shot like a proscenium.

- **Foreground left/right edges**: stone pillars of the frontier gate (steel-grey, subtle ivy growth), partially silhouette to frame
- **Center-foreground**: two main lead characters mid-stride walking outward (toward the camera-out direction). **Dawn Priest 단린** (red hair + warm robe + sword) on the left, **Pack Raider 이빨바람** (rough leather + bow on back + animal-tooth accent) on the right. Both facing slight forward, 3/4 back view (we see them stepping into the wilderness — NOT face close-up). Painterly silhouette, distinct class identifiers
- **Mid-ground beyond gate**: a wilderness path extending into pine forest, dust on the path, faint smoke or haze from a distant ridge
- **Background**: pine wilderness recedes into hazy distance, distant mountain silhouette, warm orange-rust dusk horizon
- **Sky**: dusk sky stretched across upper third, slate-blue overhead transitioning to warm orange-rust horizon

## Mood

**Threshold crossing.** The village behind is safe, the wilderness ahead is unknown. The two characters walk shoulder-to-shoulder, neither leading. A moment of stepping into the campaign. Not heroic, not despairing — quiet resolve. The gate frames them like a portrait.

## Lighting

- Dusk, sun dropped behind the distant wilderness ridges (we're looking outward from the village)
- Warm rim light on the characters from the sun direction (front-right)
- Cool slate shadow from the gate falling behind them (the village they leave behind)
- Soft atmospheric haze ahead in the wilderness — promise + threat

## Palette

- Steel-grey + weathered wood (gate, ivy) — frame
- Warm orange-rust (horizon, dust on path) — distance
- Character palette per roster (단린: warm red + cream robe; 이빨바람: warm umber + leather + animal-tooth bone-white)
- Hazy slate-blue (wilderness distance) — atmospheric
- Cool slate (village shadow behind) — leaving home tone

## Style continuity with hub baseline + character roster

The two character figures must read as **the same characters from the roster splash art** (단린 portrait + 이빨바람 portrait reference if available). Same anime-painted figure language. The gate + path + wilderness must read as the same illustrated world as `town_frontier_village:dusk`.

## What this is NOT

- NOT a character close-up portrait (3/4 back, mid-stride, framed by gate)
- NOT photoreal landscape
- NOT a battle scene
- NOT cluttered with side characters (just 2 lead + gate + path + wilderness)
- NOT dramatic god-rays or magical lighting (natural dusk only)
- NOT a different stylization from hub / roster baseline
- NOT a heroic conqueror pose — quiet shoulder-to-shoulder walk
```
