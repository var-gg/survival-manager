# UI Mockup: Theater tab — StoryArchive replay screen, dusk village backdrop

An illustrated UI mockup screenshot of a premium mobile JRPG / gacha-banner game's story archive / cutscene replay screen — anime-painted UI panel overlay on top of the frontier village hub at dusk. The Theater is where the player re-watches campaign cutscenes and dialogue events. 16:9 wide.

**STYLE LOCK**: Same illustrated world as project's character roster splash art and hub baseline. Refs: character portrait + hub `town_frontier_village:dusk`. Anime-leaning painterly base, sparse cel-edge, vivid harmonious palette. NOT photoreal, NOT wireframe.

**FIGURE GUARD**: Character ref is for stylization. The replay player frame may show a small painterly cutscene preview (suggesting characters in conversation), but no main character close-up.

## Composition

Layered: hub backdrop dimmed ~35% + frosted glass modal ~80% × ~85% centered.

### Left — Entry List (~30% of modal width)

A vertical scrolling list of story archive entries grouped by section:
- Top group: **Prologue** — 2-3 entries (each a horizontal strip: small chapter icon + placeholder title + completed checkmark + duration glyph)
- Middle group: **Chapter 1** — 4-5 entries
- Bottom group (faded, partially locked): **Chapter 2** — 2 entries with lock icons

Each entry strip has:
- Left: small thumbnail (square, painterly chapter scene snippet)
- Middle: placeholder title (abstract glyphs)
- Right: completion checkmark (gold) or lock (cool grey) + small duration glyph

The currently selected entry is highlighted with warm gold rim. A subtle scroll indicator on the right edge of the column.

### Center Main — Replay Player Frame (~45% of modal width)

A **large rectangular replay frame** (16:9 sub-aspect) showing:
- A **painterly cutscene preview snapshot** — a small scene depicting a moment from the entry: two anime-painted character figures in mid-conversation at the frontier gate at dusk, soft warm lantern light, the dusk sky behind, painterly composition like a JRPG cutscene still
- Bottom of the frame: a **playback control bar** — play/pause button (warm gold circle), scrub bar (warm gradient), time placeholder (abstract glyphs), speed toggle (1x glyph), close button

Above the frame: a thin tab bar (auto/manual/skip toggles). Below the frame: a small caption strip showing entry title + chapter context (abstract glyphs).

### Right — Entry Detail Panel (~25% of modal width)

Vertical detail panel:
- Top: **entry chapter banner** (small painterly chapter art crop)
- Middle: placeholder title + chapter info + completion date
- Below: **beat breakdown** — small list of story beats in this entry (3-5 lines, each with small icon glyph + placeholder)
- Bottom: **voice line count** + **estimated duration** + **language toggle** (small flag icons)
- Footer: **Replay CTA** button (warm gold)

## Mood

**Quiet retrospection.** The Theater feels intimate and reflective — the player returns to relive emotional beats. Warm parchment and faded gold, the dusk village paused outside. Soft frosted-glass tones. Less ceremonial than Recruit, more contemplative than Tactical Workshop.

## Lighting / Palette

- Background dim ~35%, cool slate
- Modal: warm parchment frame with frosted-glass interior
- Selected entry: warm gold rim
- Replay player frame: cinematic warm-cool contrast inside the cutscene preview
- Locked entries: cool grey faded
- Replay CTA: warm gold button (call to action)
- Painterly anime palette throughout

## What this is NOT

- NOT wireframe, NOT photoreal screenshot, NOT legible text
- NOT a character splash close-up (cutscene preview shows small figures only)
- NOT cluttered (3 sections clean)
- NOT a different stylization from the hub baseline
- NOT a real video player UI (no realistic timecode, no buffering bar)
